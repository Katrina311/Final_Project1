Music Genres

Individuals or groups can compose and perform music using a variety of instruments, including guitars, keyboards, drums, violins, and vocals."

Music as a tool to express feelings

Emotions may be expressed and evoked via music. Nostalgia, joy, grief, enthusiasm, and other emotions may all be expressed and communicated via it. A strong and universal connection to and understanding of emotions may be made via the medium of music.

Reason to like Music

Music has an innate ability to bring joy and pleasure to people's lives. The melodies, rhythms, and harmonies can be captivating and pleasurable to the ear. The sheer enjoyment derived from listening to music can be a reason why people develop a liking for it.
